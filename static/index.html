About the App
Our app, Phetch, is intended to help housemates by limiting conflict and confrontations and by keeping things organized and in one place. Some features that our app includes are finances, a chore wheel, an inventory, a shopping list, and a grocery-store-notifier.

About the Process
As a team of five, we designed a logo for our team and an app that is meant to assist housemates in living together. Since this was a design class, there was much mure emphasis on the design phase than on the implementation phase. Even though my role on the team was a designer, we all were very involved in each step of the process. Some of the design tools we used were:
  • Brainstorming ideas on butcher paper as a group
  • Individually sketching ideas
  • Personas and storyboards
  • Multiple prototypes (paper and digital) and user testing
  • Documenting our progress in a blog
